Circuit 1: HC-SR04 Ultrasonic Sensor

Component Pin Arduino Pin Wire Color Function
HC-SR04 VCC 5V Red/Yellow Power Supply
HC-SR04 GND GND Black Ground
HC-SR04 Trig D7 Green Trigger Signal
HC-SR04 Echo D6 Orange Echo Response

Circuit 2: DHT22 Temperature & Humidity Sensor

Component Pin Arduino Pin Wire Color Function
DHT22 VCC 5V Red/Yellow Power Supply
DHT22 Data D2 Orange Data Communication
DHT22 NC - - Not Connected
DHT22 GND GND Black Ground
Important: The 10kΩ pull-up resistor between Data pin and 5V is essential for proper DHT22 operation. Some DHT22 breakout boards have this resistor built-in.

Circuit 3: LED and 5V Motor Control

Component Connection Arduino Pin Notes
LED Anode Through 220Ω resistor D9 Current limiting resistor required
LED Cathode Direct GND Ground connection
Motor + Through transistor 5V Switched by transistor
Motor - Transistor emitter GND Through transistor to ground
Transistor Base Direct D3 Motor control signal
Safety Warning: Never connect a motor directly to Arduino digital pins! Use a transistor or MOSFET driver circuit as shown above with a flyback diode to protect against voltage spikes.
